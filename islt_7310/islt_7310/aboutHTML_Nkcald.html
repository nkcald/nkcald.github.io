Doris Rogers

From MLIS Student to Lbrarian

I am Doris Rogers and six years ago, I started working as a part-time substitute clerk in a small community library. Since that time, I have worked in public service, cataloging, and have written several successful library technology grants. I have a Bachelor of Science degree in Criminal Justice Administration and a Master of Public Administration.
I am currently pursuing my Master of Library and Information Science (MLIS) degree, without specialization, however, I plan to continue to work in the public library sector"proceed as if success is inevitable" by an unknown author and this has been my motto for the last two years.

Onward and Upward!
